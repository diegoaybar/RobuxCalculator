How to Use the Robux Tax Calculator

The Robux Tax Calculator helps Roblox players calculate how much Robux they’ll receive after the 30% Roblox tax or how much they need to give to get a specific amount after tax. Enter an amount in "Robux Given" to see what you’ll receive, or in "Robux to Receive" to see how much to give. Results update automatically as you type!
